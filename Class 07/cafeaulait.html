Cafe au Lait by Martha Stewart

Please input the number of servings you wish to make and then press the 'Party Size' button and your ingredient amounts will appear:

cups of warmed milk

cups freshly brewed coffee

Instructions for a party of 2 by Martha Stewart:

  1. Heat 1 to 1.5 cups milk in a saucepan over medium-low heat, whisking, until milk is steaming and slightly foamy.
  2. Divide milk between two cafe au lait bowls or large coffee cups. Divide 2 cups coffee between bowls and stir.

This recipe uses three quarters of a cup of milk per person, which is what Martha recommends to make light cafe au lait, see here (http://www.marthastewart.com/1142721/cafe-ai-lait)
