Biography

My favorite movie that has received an Academy Award is Big Hero 6. The movie is centralized around Hiro, who is a Robotics prodigy spending too much time on illegal robot fights. His older brother Tadashi attempts to direct Hiro's wasted talents by showing him his University lab where he created Baymax, a robot with the sole purpose of taking care of people. Hiro is then encouraged to invent something to present to the robotics convention in hopes of gaining entrance into the university. After a fire breaks out during the convention, killing Tadashi, Hiro teams up with Go Go, Honey Lemon, Wasabi, Fred, and the lovable Baymax to find out who was responsible for the fire. The lead actors and actresses in the movie were Ryan Potter (Hiro), Jamie Chung (Go Go), Genesis Rodriguez (Honey Lemon), Scott Admsit (Baymax), T.J. Miller (Fred), and Damon Wayans Jr. (Wasabi). Big Hero 6 is my favorite movie because although the story was tragic, there were moments of appropriate placed humour from Baymax due to the character being hilarious when he has low battery. Also the animation was amazing and was able to fuse the cities of San Francisco and Tokyo together seamlessly to create San Fransokyo.

The second movie I chose as my favorite as an Academy Award winner would be Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse. The movie revolves around Miles Morales, a teenager living in Brooklyn. He gets bitten by a radioactive spider and begins developing abilities that turn him into the one and only Spider-Man. Miles then meets Peter Parker and his other Spider-Man counterparts from alternate realities. Together, they team up in order to prevent Kingpin, a giant madman who is dead-set on opening portals to other universes, before it destroys every dimension.

Image from:Coming Soon Network.

The relationship Big Hero 6and Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse is that both were winners of the Best Animated Feature when they were nominated. Also, both movies were focused on the theme of superheroes even though it was less prominent in Big Hero 6. The lead actors and actresses for Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse were Shameik Moore (Miles Morales), Hailee Steinfeld (Gwen Stacy), and Jake Johnson (Spider-Man). To compare Big Hero 6and Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse, both were created by large studios: Disney and Sony. Also, the movies are both based on comic books, making heavily anticipated by the public. Something that sets the movies apart from one another is that in Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse, there are elements of a classic comic book. An example of this would be when some of the scenes are animated and colored using the pop art style.
